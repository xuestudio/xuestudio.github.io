Our Classes

Our RAD Ballet Classes follow the RAD syllabus from grades 1-5 and intermediate. Ballet is the foundation from nearly all dance style. We offer scientific and progressively ballet training, RAD Ballet Classes are strongly recommended for students who thinking about a career in the performing arts.

In Hip Hop Classes, boys and girls will learn basic hip hop moves and choreography in the supportive and encouraging class, building confidence and strengthening creativity. There are lots of dancing, lots of cool things, lots of fun!
Our Preschool Dance & Music Classes begin at 3 years old, both girls and boys are welcomed. We teach a combination of music and dance, forming little ones the foundation of dance technique and the sense of music. The children will develop physical skills, self-expression, creativity and imagination through the classes.
This class is the private class. Please contact us for more information.
